About Me

As a penultimate undergraduate student passionate about AI agents and 3D Vision, I am currently involved in two key projects: Machine Learning for Molecular Prediction - training models to predict inhibitors of Tdp1, contributing to drug discovery with members from the Faculty of Medicine of CUHK; Computer Vision for Spatial Understanding - the final year project under Prof. Chi-Wing FU, Philip, focusing on mixed reality for surgical navigation and training. I am particularly interested in how intelligent agents can be integrated into the exploration of the physical and digital world.
